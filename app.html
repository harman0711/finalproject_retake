OUR APP

Genesis is a very simple product. It doesn’t consist of a ton of different functions, but focuses on the most important ones. These are being able to show anyone how much C02 their clothes takes to be produced and transported, their water waste and how the employees are being treated. We want people to beware of their purchases and be educated on their choices

THE FUNCTIONALITY

Ones you have logged in. You are greeted with 3 options. The first one which is called scan my product, will give you a summary of your exact product. The second options lets the user look back at the scanned products so that the user doesn’t have to type the apparel again. The third options takes the user to their profile, where the user is able to change their password, read our terms and services and be directed to our customer service.

THE CALCULATION

The main function that our application offers is the ability to identify if the type of clothing is overall bad or good. How does our products do so? By inputting information such as brand, type of clothing and serial number you will get an overall look of that exact clothing apparel. By collecting data from the many brands on our planet we are able to give someone like you a better understanding of how many resources and effort it took.

THE RESULT

All of the information about the apparel you either have scanned using your qr-scanner or manually typed using the serial number. Will now appear on the app. The item will be given an overall rating. Virtually you can see the rating by looking at the stars given or the percentage. You can see the criteria such as amount of Co2 emissions that was let out while producing and transporting the product, water waste and how the brand treates the workers.

MY PRODUCTS

All the previous scanned products will appear on this screen. This page show you how sustainable the products is. To indicate how good or bad it is we use emoji’s with different kinds of emotions. If the product is overall bad it will be shown a sad face and if it is overall good there will be shown a smiley face.

TO THE FUTURE AND BEYOND

As of today, many brands doesn’t have the capability to scan their clothing using qr-code. But many companies want to change and become modern. We have a goal that all clothes being sold in Norway should have a qr-code tag by 2025. Until we are able to use this new scan technology, you have to manually type the clothing information to get the background details.
